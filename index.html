How I Help

Support for families, production companies, and expedition teams

Every assignment is unique. Below are the core ways I can plug into your world — whether you are a private family, a travel program, or a TV/film production with staff and children on location.

For private families

  • Traveling tutor / governess for 1–3 children
  • Custom curriculum aligned with home country standards
  • Daily routines, structure & gentle accountability
  • College prep, essays & long-term planning

For productions

  • On-set educator for minors
  • Academic continuity during shoots
  • Talent support: emotional & routine stability
  • Basecamp assistance & light logistics

For expeditions

  • Field instructor for teen & student trips
  • Briefings, reflection sessions & journaling
  • Risk-aware supervision and group cohesion
  • Community & cultural learning modules

For youth

  • Mindset coaching during big transitions
  • Working with anxiety, perfectionism, self-doubt
  • Personal leadership & communication skills
  • Support for first-gen & high-achieving students
Ideal assignments

Multi-week or multi-month placements where young people need both academic guidance and a steady adult presence as their world changes around them. I’m especially well-suited to:

  • Families traveling between homes or on sabbatical
  • TV/film crews based in remote or island locations
  • Ship- or yacht-based schooling arrangements
  • Teen travel programs & educational expeditions

I’m happy to discuss unique setups and help you design the right educational and support structure for your specific situation.
